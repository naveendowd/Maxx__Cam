Tonny Andersen Soby A/S

Here at Søby AS, we use MaxxCAM for our Pullmax turret punching machines and our Bystronic lasers. For us, it has been a great change; we save a lot of time with nesting and have significantly better utilization of sheets than we had before.

Jeremy Steel NQ Sheetmetal Pvt Ltd

We were searching for a CAD/CAM package that was both easy to learn and use for our new AMADA EMZ-3510 turret punch. After testing various software options, we found that MaxxCAM was the perfect fit for our business due to its intuitive interface and logical workflow.

Cosmin Cojocaru SBS Kuhltechnik GmbH

At SBS Kühltechnik GmbH, we have recently implemented a new Trumpf TruPunch 1000 (S19) to work alongside our existing Trumpf TC500R. The new Trumpf TruPunch 1000 (S19) is equipped with advanced functions that have greatly improved our fabrication needs. When we wanted to move from programming the same parts over and over again to building families of products that could be reused as parametric templates, we chose MaxxCAM.
