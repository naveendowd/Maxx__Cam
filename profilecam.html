Maximise the potential of laser, plasma, oxy-fuel and water-jet cutting machines with MaxxCAM’s cutting module. The sophisticated technology optimizes machine parameters after analyzing parts.

Features include:

  • Unsurpassed support for profile/cutting machines
  • Support for Punch/laser/plasma combination machines
  • Automatic assignment of lead-in and lead-out strategies
  • Automatic selection of piercing types based on geometry complexity
  • Automatic optimization of travel and cutting path
  • Automatic placement of micro-joints
  • Automatic repositioning and splitting of laser cuts
  • Automatic hole destruction
  • Automatic plastic film vaporization
  • Automatic engraving
  • Automatic sheet cropping
  • Automatic part removal by chute or picker device
  • Cost estimation for parts and nests
